Chocolate Chip Cookies!

This is my go-to chocolate chip cookie recipe. Pro tip, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top while the cookie is still warm!

Allrecipes' Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients
1 cup of softened butter
1 cup of white sugar
1 cup of packed brown sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
1 teaspoon of baking soda
2 teaspoons of hot water
1/2 teaspoons salt
3 cups of all-purpose flour
2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Beat together white sugar, brown sugar and butter in a large bowl until smooth.
2. Beat in eggs, one at a time. Add vanilla.
3. Mix baking soda and hot water in a seperate bowl, then add to batter with salt. Stir in flour and chocolate chips.
4. Place spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
5. Cook for about 10 minutes, or until golden brown. Enjoy!
